MALL COPS

The Mall Cops event is a meme competition that promises a lighthearted and entertaining experience for its 12 skilled participants. The goal of the event is for each participant to score points by successfully taking down other PMCs and completing Exfils. However, the challenge is heightened by the fact that SCAVs will be spawning during the event, making it even more difficult for competitors to survive and move through locations undetected. The Mall Cops event is not just about scoring points, but also about embracing the humor and lightheartedness of the competition. Participants are encouraged to fully immerse themselves in the theme of the event, and to approach the competition with a sense of humor and fun. In summary, the Mall Cops event promises to be a hilarious and exciting experience for all involved. From the specific loadout to the spawning of SCAVs, every aspect of the competition has been designed to challenge and entertain participants. While the rules are specific, the spirit of the event is lighthearted and fun, and participants are encouraged to embrace the humor of the competition and enjoy the experience to the fullest.

Overall, the Mall Cops is a unique solo competition that tests participants' skills with specific weapons and strategy in an intense, pump-action environment.

Note: This is an Interchange only event.

Note: Players may NOT use Secure Containers of ANY type in this event.

RESTRICTED AMMUNITION

To safeguard the overall viewing experience and elevate the significance of headshots, we have enforced restrictions on ammunition. By limiting the use of certain ammunition, players are encouraged to engage in longer and more calculated combat scenarios. This promotes a more thoughtful and methodical approach to the game, as players must rely on their skill and positioning rather than relying solely on powerful ammunition. Skilled positioning and strategic tactics become more valuable in this environment, allowing players to earn their victories through well-executed plans and careful gameplay. These restrictions not only promote a more challenging and immersive experience but also add an extra layer of depth and strategy to the gameplay. Overall, the intention of these restrictions is to create a more engaging and rewarding experience for players who are willing to put in the effort and skill required to succeed. All restricted and allowed ammunition is viewable on the spreadsheet below.

RESTRICTED ATTACHMENTS

To establish a balanced and equitable gaming environment, we have implemented limitations on thermal sights and UBGLs. This guarantees that all PMCs must depend on their expertise and abilities to identify and eliminate potential dangers. Participants will not be permitted to rely on an attachment to perform this task for them.

No thermal signts.
No Under Barrel Grenade Launchers.
No Magazines over a 50rnd Capacity.
No 7.62x51mm 50rnd Magazines.
No 7.62x39mm 50rnd Magazines.

STIMULANT RULES

To uphold the integrity of the tournament, we have removed the use of all stimulants except for Morphine. Players may take stimulants off of bodies without restriction.
