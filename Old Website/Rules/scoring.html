Scoring

Global Event Scoring System

Competitors, welcome to our scoring system, where your performance will be evaluated based on two key actions: PMC kills and successful exfils.

First, let's talk about PMC kills. You will earn three points for each player-controlled character you eliminate during a match. This will be the primary way to score points and increase your position on the scoreboard. So, the more PMC kills you achieve, the higher your score will be!

Next, let's discuss successful exfils. You will earn five points for each successful extraction from a match. So remember, getting out alive is just as important as taking down your enemies!

It is important to note that SCAV kills, boss kills, and loot do not contribute to your score. Our scoring system is designed to focus on the actions that are most relevant to the events objectives, PvP. This means that accumulating wealth or other game resources will not help you win the competition.

In conclusion, our scoring system is designed to provide a fair and objective evaluation of your performance in a head to head competition in our events. By focusing on PMC kills and successful exfils, we can accurately assess your combat and survival skills against your fellow community members. Good luck!
