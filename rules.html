Reporting Offenders

Suspected cheat abuse generally refers to a situation where someone is suspected of cheating or engaging in unfair practices in a particular setting. In the context of gaming, cheat abuse can include using third-party software to gain an unfair advantage over other players.

Teaming generally refers to a situation where players form a team or alliance with each other, typically in a game mode where players are supposed to compete against each other individually. For example, in a game like Tarkov, teaming could involve players working together to take out other players, even though they are not on the same team.

Certain items, weapons, and armor are restricted in our tournaments in order to create a level playing field for all players. Using restricted items in these situations will result in penalties or disqualification.
