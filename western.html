WESTERN SHOWDOWN

The Western Showdown is a thrilling and captivating solo competition that transports its 12 skilled participants back to the era of the old west. With the aim of scoring points by successfully taking down other PMCs and completing Exfils, the event promises to be an exciting and challenging experience for all participants. One of the unique aspects of this competition is that SCAVs will not spawn during the event, thereby creating an environment that fosters tactical interactions between players. This means that players will have to rely solely on their own abilities and strategies to emerge victorious. To ensure a level playing field, all participants are required to use a specific loadout that has been carefully curated for the event. The loadout will be outlined in detail to all participants prior to the start of the competition, and strict adherence to the guidelines is expected. The Western Showdown is not just any ordinary competition - it is a meme event that promises to be a hilarious and entertaining experience for all involved. Participants are encouraged to embrace the theme of the old west and fully immerse themselves in the spirit of the event. In summary, The Western Showdown is an event that is guaranteed to provide participants with a unique and unforgettable experience. From the carefully curated loadout to the absence of SCAVs, every aspect of the competition has been designed to challenge and entertain players in equal measure.

Overall, the Western Showdown is a unique solo competition that tests participants' skills with specific weapons and strategy in an intense, break action environment.

Note: This is a Customs only event.

Note: Players may NOT use Secure Containers of ANY type in this event.

REQUIRED AMMUNITION

To adhere to the spirit of this event we are limiting the use of ammunition. You may only use 7.56x54R LPS, 12G Express Buckshot, and .357 FMJ amunition. No other ammunition is allowed.

ATTACHMENT RULES

To establish a balanced and equitable gaming environment, we have implemented limitations on thermal sights and UBGLs. This guarantees that all PMCs must depend on their expertise and abilities to identify and eliminate potential dangers. Participants will not be permitted to rely on an attachment to perform this task for them.

No thermal signts.
No Under Barrel Grenade Launchers.
No Magazines over a 50rnd Capacity.
No 7.62x51mm 50rnd Magazines.
No 7.62x39mm 50rnd Magazines.

STIMULANT RESTRICTIONS

To uphold the integrity of the tournament, we have removed the use of all stimulants except for Morphine. Players may take stimulants off of bodies without restriction.
