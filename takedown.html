TAKEDOWN TOURNAMENT

The Takedown tournament is an intense solo competition that brings together 12 skilled participants, each with the goal of scoring points by successfully taking down other PMCs and successfully completing Exfils. To add to the challenge, SCAVs will spawn during the event, making it even more difficult for competitors to survive and traverse locations quietly. Despite the competition's nature, the rules are relatively simple. There are minimal restrictions on ammo, armor, weapons, and attachments, allowing competitors to choose the gear they feel most comfortable using. This means that participants will have to rely on their personal skills, strategy, and tactics to secure a win.

Overall, the Takedown tournament is a thrilling solo competition that tests participants' skills and strategy in an intense, high-pressure environment. The minimal restrictions and dynamic raid order create a unique challenge for competitors and ensure that each participant has an equal opportunity to come out on top.

Note: This is a Customs only event.

Note: Players may NOT use Secure Containers of ANY type in this event.

AMMUNITION RESTRICTIONS

To safeguard the overall viewing experience and elevate the significance of headshots, we have enforced restrictions on ammunition. By limiting the use of certain ammunition, players are encouraged to engage in longer and more calculated combat scenarios. This promotes a more thoughtful and methodical approach to the game, as players must rely on their skill and positioning rather than relying solely on powerful ammunition. Skilled positioning and strategic tactics become more valuable in this environment, allowing players to earn their victories through well-executed plans and careful gameplay. These restrictions not only promote a more challenging and immersive experience but also add an extra layer of depth and strategy to the gameplay. Overall, the intention of these restrictions is to create a more engaging and rewarding experience for players who are willing to put in the effort and skill required to succeed. All restricted and allowed ammunition is viewable on the spreadsheet below.

RESTRICTED ATTACHMENTS

To establish a balanced and equitable gaming environment, we have implemented limitations on thermal sights and UBGLs. This guarantees that all PMCs must depend on their expertise and abilities to identify and eliminate potential dangers. Participants will not be permitted to rely on an attachment to perform this task for them.

No thermal signts.
No Under Barrel Grenade Launchers.
No Magazines over a 50rnd Capacity.
No 7.62x51mm 50rnd Magazines.
No 7.62x39mm 50rnd Magazines.

RESTRICTED ARMOR

In order to preserve the advantage of precisely aimed shots and incentivize players for their strategic maneuvers, we are placing limitations on the utilization of top-tier helmets.

No Altyn bulletproof helmet.
No Rys-T bulletproof helmet.
No Vulkan-5 (LShZ-5).
No Killa's Helmet.
No Tagilla's Helmet.
No Bastion Class 6 Plate.

STIMULANT RULES

To uphold the integrity of the tournament, we have implemented restrictions on the consumption of stimulants by players. The list of approved stimulants is limited to those specified below, and there are restrictions in place to ensure that players still face the effects of stamina depletion, the need to use medkits, and are motivated to play in a more realistic and conservative manner. Please refer to the approved stimulants and their corresponding limitations outlined below for more information. Players may take stimulants off of bodies without restriction.

Morphine has no limit on uses.
Competitors can only carry a total of 2 Propital/Blue Blood.
Competitors can only carry a total of 1 ETG stim.
Competitors can only carry a total of 1 Zagustin stim.
Competitors can only carry a total of 1 SJ6 stim.
No Bastion Class 6 Plate.
