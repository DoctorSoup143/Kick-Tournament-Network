Takedown Tournament Ruleset

About

The Limitless Legion is an intense online solo competition that sends 12 skilled participants into online raids, each with the goal of scoring points by successfully taking down other PMCs and successfully completing Exfils. Despite the competition's nature, the rules are relatively simple. There are no on ammo, armor, weapons, minimal restrictions on attachments, and no restrictions on armor allowing competitors to choose the gear they feel most comfortable using.

Note: This is a Customs only event.


Ammunition

There are no restrictions on ammunition.




Attachments

To establish a balanced and equitable gaming environment, we have implemented limitations on thermal sights and UBGLs. This guarantees that all PMCs must depend on their expertise and abilities to identify and eliminate potential dangers. Participants will not be permitted to rely on an attachment to perform this task for them.

Armor Pieces
