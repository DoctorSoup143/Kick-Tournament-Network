Kalashnikov Clash Rules

About

The Kalashnikov Clash is a solo event that includes the maps Customs, Interchange, Reserve, and Shorline. During the event, Customs will be played twice - once at the beginning and once at the end. This free-for-all competition will feature 12 competitors vying for the highest score. Points will be awarded for both survival and PMC kills.


Ammunition

By limiting the use of certain ammunition, players are encouraged to engage in longer and more calculated combat scenarios. This promotes a more thoughtful and methodical approach to the game, as players must rely on their skill and positioning rather than relying solely on powerful ammunition. Skilled positioning and strategic tactics become more valuable in this environment, allowing players to earn their victories through well-executed plans and careful gameplay. These restrictions not only promote a more challenging and immersive experience but also add an extra layer of depth and strategy to the gameplay. Overall, the intention of these restrictions is to create a more engaging and rewarding experience for players who are willing to put in the effort and skill required to succeed. Attachments

Magazines above a 30rnd capacity are restricted in this event type to apply a level of resource management to ammunition and magazines. All types of thermal optics are also restricted. UBGL (Under Barrel Grenade Launchers) are also restricted from use in this event.




Armor Pieces

For all events, there is a list of armor, tactical rig, and helmet pieces that have been restricted. Participants should carefully review the list to ensure that they are not planning to use any of the restricted equipment during the event. Any armor, tac rig, or helmet that is included in the restricted list will not be permitted for use unless found in raid.
