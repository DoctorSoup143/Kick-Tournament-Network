AK Platform Events

Banned Ammunition

By limiting the use of certain ammunition, players are encouraged to engage in longer and more calculated combat scenarios. This promotes a more thoughtful and methodical approach to the game, as players must rely on their skill and positioning rather than relying solely on powerful ammunition. Skilled positioning and strategic tactics become more valuable in this environment, allowing players to earn their victories through well-executed plans and careful gameplay. These restrictions not only promote a more challenging and immersive experience but also add an extra layer of depth and strategy to the gameplay. Overall, the intention of these restrictions is to create a more engaging and rewarding experience for players who are willing to put in the effort and skill required to succeed.

Approved Weapons

To ensure fairness and safety in the event, a list of approved AK platform weapons has been established. Participants are advised to check the list to ensure that the firearm they intend to use is included. Any AK platform weapon not included in the approved list will not be allowed during the event. This measure aims to provide a level playing field for all participants while also promoting safety during the competition.
