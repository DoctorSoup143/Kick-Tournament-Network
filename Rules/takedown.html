Takedown Tournament Ruleset

About

The Takedown tournament is an intense solo competition that brings together 12 skilled participants, each with the goal of scoring points by successfully taking down other PMCs and successfully completing Exfils. To add to the challenge, SCAVs will spawn during the event, making it even more difficult for competitors to survive and traverse locations quietly. Despite the competition's nature, the rules are relatively simple. There are minimal restrictions on ammo, armor, weapons, and attachments, allowing competitors to choose the gear they feel most comfortable using. This means that participants will have to rely on their personal skills, strategy, and tactics to secure a win.

The tournament consists of five raids that will take place in a predetermined order. The first raid will take place in customs, followed by an Interchange raid, a shoreline raid, a reserve raid, and then a final customs raid. This order ensures that competitors will face different challenges in each raid, providing a unique and dynamic experience throughout the tournament.

Overall, the Takedown tournament is a thrilling solo competition that tests participants' skills and strategy in an intense, high-pressure environment. The minimal restrictions and dynamic raid order create a unique challenge for competitors and ensure that each participant has an equal opportunity to come out on top.
