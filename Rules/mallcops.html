Mall Cops Ruleset

About

The Mall Cops event is an humor filled meme competition that brings together 12 skilled participants, each with the goal of scoring points by successfully taking down other PMCs and successfully completing Exfils. To add to the challenge, SCAVs will spawn during the event, making it even more difficult for competitors to survive and traverse locations quietly. Despite the competition's nature, the rules are restricted and specific. Competitors will be restricted to a specific loadout for the Mall Cops event. This means that participants will have to rely on their personal skills, strategy, and tactics to secure a win.

Overall, the Mall Cops is a unique solo competition that tests participants' skills with specific weapons and strategy in an intense, pump-action environment.

Note: This is an Interchange only event.

Note: Players may NOT use Secure Containers of ANY type in this event.

Ammunition

To safeguard the overall viewing experience and elevate the significance of headshots, we have enforced restrictions on ammunition. By limiting the use of certain ammunition, players are encouraged to engage in longer and more calculated combat scenarios. This promotes a more thoughtful and methodical approach to the game, as players must rely on their skill and positioning rather than relying solely on powerful ammunition. Skilled positioning and strategic tactics become more valuable in this environment, allowing players to earn their victories through well-executed plans and careful gameplay. These restrictions not only promote a more challenging and immersive experience but also add an extra layer of depth and strategy to the gameplay. Overall, the intention of these restrictions is to create a more engaging and rewarding experience for players who are willing to put in the effort and skill required to succeed. All restricted and allowed ammunition is viewable on this spreadsheet.




Attachments

To establish a balanced and equitable gaming environment, we have implemented limitations on thermal sights and UBGLs. This guarantees that all PMCs must depend on their expertise and abilities to identify and eliminate potential dangers. Participants will not be permitted to rely on an attachment to perform this task for them.
